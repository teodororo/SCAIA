## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in this project, please report it responsibly by sending an email to **xxxxx@gmail.com**.

Please include a clear description of the issue, steps to reproduce it, and any relevant details that may help us understand the impact and scope.

You can expect an initial response within **48 hours**. We will keep you informed of our progress and let you know whether the vulnerability has been accepted or declined within **7 days**. If accepted, we will work on a fix and may credit you in the release notes upon your consent.

We kindly ask that you do not publicly disclose the issue until we have had a chance to address it.
